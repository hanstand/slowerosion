We Grow Up Into Ghosts

A careful and methodical exploration of grief. This iterative video series was born out of video manipulation software that I created to distort live recordings. By averaging the pixels from a live-stream over time, human form becomes ghost; past, present, and future are collapsed into a single continuum. A dancer walking is shown with all her previous steps trailing behind her; a dancer who is moving too quickly will disappear altogether.
