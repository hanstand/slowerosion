Your Dreams Are Growing Teeth

Four intricate, methodical solos crafted on parallel tracks. Your Dreams Are Growing Teeth uses mathematical structures and task-based improvisations to organize a dance. It sits at the complex intersection of logical systems and irrational humanity. Made in collaboration with the performers. Presented at Velocity Dance Center.

Supported by       Velocity's Bridge Project

Performed by       Becca Blackwell, Corina Dalzell, Ciara McCormack, & El Nyberg
